Getting Started

Smart Notes Organizer is a powerful tool that helps you convert your handwritten or printed notes into well-organized digital format using AI technology.

  1. Visit the home page
  2. Upload your image using drag & drop or click to select
  3. Wait for the AI processing to complete
  4. Get your enhanced, organized notes in markdown format

Uploading Images

You can upload images in two ways:

  • Drag and drop your image directly onto the upload area
  • Click the upload area to select an image from your device

Supported image formats:

  • PNG
  • JPG
  • JPEG

Processing

Our AI-powered processing includes:

  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text
  • AI enhancement for better organization
  • Markdown formatting for clean output

Output Format

Your notes will be converted to markdown format with:

  • Clear headings and subheadings
  • Properly formatted lists
  • Code blocks (if present)
  • Tables (if present)

Tips & Tricks

  • Ensure good lighting when taking photos of notes
  • Keep the image clear and focused
  • Avoid shadows and glare on the paper
  • Use the copy button to quickly copy the output
