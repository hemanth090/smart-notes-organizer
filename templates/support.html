Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, and JPEG formats. Make sure your images are clear and well-lit for best results.

How long does processing take?

Processing typically takes a few seconds, depending on the image size and complexity of the notes.

What if the text recognition is poor?

Try ensuring good lighting and clear handwriting. You can also try taking a closer photo or scanning the document.

Can I edit the enhanced notes?

Yes, you can copy the text and edit it in your preferred markdown editor or text processor.
